Ajwah Dates are the most popular among Muslims
today and there are many important reasons why,
they are a delightfully soft and fruity,
quite dark in color and tend to be a more of
dry variety of date from Saudi Arabia,
that is actually cultivated in Madinah Munawwara.

Sukkari Dates are the Queen of Dates that are known
for their golden, soft, melt-in-your-mouth goodness.
Sukkari dates are the perfect replacement to sugary treats.
